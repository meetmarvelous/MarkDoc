Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 7, 2026

1. Your Privacy Matters

MarkDocx AI is designed with privacy in mind. All document processing happens entirely in your browser — your content is never sent to any server.

2. Data Storage

Your documents are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly download a file. Clearing your browser data will remove your saved documents.

3. No Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking mechanisms. Your writing activity is completely private.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following external services for functionality:

  • Google Fonts — for typography
  • jsDelivr CDN — for JavaScript libraries

These services may have their own privacy policies regarding how they handle requests.

5. Questions

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
